# Bee Twist
Combination of two popular anagram games: Text Twist and NYT Spelling Bee.

_Disclaimer_: This app clones some of the behavior of both games so that I can explore various technologies and AI-enabled development.
- Text Twist was developed by GameHouse
- Spelling Bee was developed by The New York Times Games

## Concept
One feature of Text Twist that I wish Spelling Bee had was the ability to see how many words hadn't been guessed yet and their lengths. This guides the user to more targetted guesses and ultimately helps them learn what words are in the dictionary of accepted words. This latter trait helps them build skills to perform better on future puzzles.

## Professional purpose
At the time of writing, my job role didn't involve much coding or direct AI-enabled workflows. In short, I knew _of_ lots of things but had little experience _applying_ those things.

This small app helps me keep my skills sharp, explore areas that are new to me, and consider how to apply AI in the SDLC (e.g., skills, agents).
